Identification of soil

Indian farmers can identify soil types by assessing color, texture, and structure. Red soils indicate iron content, while black soils are rich in minerals. Testing helps tailor crop choices for optimal yield.

Seeding

Seeding involves planting seeds in soil to initiate crop growth. Proper spacing, depth, and timing are crucial for successful germination. Adequate water and nutrients support healthy seedlings, ensuring robust plant development.

Care Of Crop

Crop care involves regular monitoring for pests and diseases, appropriate irrigation, and timely application of fertilizers. Weeding, pruning, and protecting against adverse weather conditions contribute to maximizing yield and quality.

Fertilization enhances soil fertility by supplying essential nutrients to plants. Balanced use promotes healthy growth, increased yields, and sustainable agriculture practices.
